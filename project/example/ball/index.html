Flexiv ball balancing challenge

Apr 27, 2016
demo

Objectives

The goal of this project is to control the motion of the robot end-effector such that the ball on the mounted plate of the robot can track a circle or the company’s logo. The position and velocity of the ball on the plate are sensed and estimated based on the force-torque sensor reading. The control development is a specialized MPC design based on my research. The work is implemented using the whole-body control framework, and signals are properly filtered with IIR low-pass filter to achieve precise motion.

Demo

This can be found on youtube under the flexiv ball balancing challenge. The demo can be found here demo video. Note that the robot shown in the video is running in QNX, and the work was mostly developed and tested under real-time Linux.
